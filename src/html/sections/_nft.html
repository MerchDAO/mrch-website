NFT stands for non-fungible (unique) tokens.
It's a technology that allows to record and authenticate ownership on the blockchain.
With NFTs, we create a unique digital identity for every piece sold on MerchDAO.

An NFT with every order

  • Every item on MerchDAO has its own NFT token
  • Scan the QR code on the box to access NFT info
  • If you own the NFT token, you own the item
